# Syndication Quick Checklist
*For each new SleepSmarter.io article*

## Pre-Syndication (Week 1)
- [ ] Article published on SleepSmarter.io
- [ ] Wait 1-2 weeks for Google indexing
- [ ] Identify 3-5 target Quora questions
- [ ] Prepare Medium-adapted version

## Medium Syndication (Week 2)
- [ ] Use Medium "Import story" feature
- [ ] Set canonical URL: `https://sleepsmarter.io/[article-url]`
- [ ] Add engaging featured image
- [ ] Format for Medium (short paragraphs, subheadings)
- [ ] Add 5 relevant tags:
  - [ ] Sleep
  - [ ] Health
  - [ ] [Topic-specific tag 1]
  - [ ] [Topic-specific tag 2]
  - [ ] [Topic-specific tag 3]
- [ ] Include CTA back to SleepSmarter.io
- [ ] Submit to relevant Medium publications
- [ ] Share on social media

## Quora Syndication (Week 3)
**Answer these high-traffic questions (1-2 per day):**

### Top 10 Sleep Questions:
- [ ] "What are some effective ways to fall asleep quickly?"
- [ ] "How can I stop waking up in the middle of the night?"
- [ ] "What are the best natural remedies for insomnia?"
- [ ] "How does sleep apnea affect overall health?"
- [ ] "What's the ideal temperature for sleeping?"
- [ ] "How many hours of sleep do I really need?"
- [ ] "What are the best foods to eat for better sleep?"
- [ ] "How can I improve my sleep quality without medication?"
- [ ] "What's the connection between stress and sleep?"
- [ ] "Are sleep tracking devices actually accurate?"

### For each answer:
- [ ] Provide 2-3 actionable tips from article
- [ ] Include natural link to full article
- [ ] Add disclosure: "I'm part of SleepSmarter.io"
- [ ] Engage with any comments
- [ ] Upvote helpful related answers

## Tracking
- [ ] Add UTM parameters to all links
- [ ] Monitor GA4 for syndication traffic
- [ ] Track newsletter signups from syndicated content
- [ ] Note which questions/answers perform best

---

**UTM Parameters:**
- Medium: `?utm_source=medium&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=syndication`
- Quora: `?utm_source=quora&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=syndication`

**Canonical URL Format:**
`https://sleepsmarter.io/[original-article-path]`
