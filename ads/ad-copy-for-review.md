## CHANGES FROM v1 (Why we rewrote):
1. **Removed "personalized sleep plan" claims** — calculator gives same results for same wake time. Now accurately describes what it does.
2. **Removed "Used by 10,000+ people"** — unverified social proof claim. Removed entirely.
3. **Removed "based on your age, lifestyle, and wake time"** — calculator only uses wake time. Now accurate.
4. **Added assessment references** — we're building a real 10-question personalized assessment, so ads now reference it honestly.
5. **Changed "personalized" to "tailored to your sleep habits"** — reflects the assessment-based recommendations we're actually building.
6. **All claims now match what the product actually delivers.**

## REVIEW NOTES

**Please evaluate for:**
1. **Headline effectiveness** — Are they compelling? Do they stand out from competitors?
2. **Description clarity** — Clear value prop? Strong CTA?
3. **Keyword-ad relevance** — Do headlines match search intent for the target keywords?
4. **Compliance** — Any claims that could violate Google Ads health policies?
5. **Character limits** — Headlines max 30 chars, descriptions max 90 chars
6. **Honesty check** — Do all claims accurately reflect what the product delivers?
7. **Differentiation** — What makes these ads stand out vs generic sleep content?
8. **Missing angles** — Any emotional triggers or benefits we're not hitting?
