**Version:** 2.0 (Post-Audit Rebuild)  
**Goal:** Reduce Month 3 churn from >50% to <30%  
**Approach:** Multi-layered automated retention system

## Retention Philosophy

### Core Principles
1. **Prevention > Intervention:** Build retention into the product experience
2. **Value Stacking:** Multiple layers of value that compound over time
3. **Sunk Cost Engineering:** Progress becomes psychological investment
4. **Friction Timing:** Right friction at right time (not at signup)
5. **Graceful Degradation:** Cancellation doesn't mean complete loss

### Churn Risk Profile (Previous Model)
- **Month 1:** 15% churn (onboarding failure)
- **Month 2:** 25% churn (novelty wears off)
- **Month 3:** 50%+ churn (value not perceived)
- **Month 6:** 70%+ churn (no progression)

### Target Churn Profile (New Model)
- **Month 1:** <10% churn (improved onboarding)
- **Month 3:** <30% churn (progression + value)
- **Month 6:** <40% churn (habit + community)
- **Month 12:** <50% churn (annual commitment)
- **LTV Increase:** $228+ (12+ months)

## The Multi-Step Cancellation Flow

### Step 0: Prevention (Before Cancel Click)
**Engagement Detection:** 7+ days inactivity triggers "We miss you" sequence
**Value Reminder:** Monthly progress report highlights achievements
**Annual Upsell:** "Lock in savings" offer at 3-month mark

### Step 1: The "What's Wrong?" Survey (Cancel Click)
**Trigger:** Member clicks "Cancel Membership"
**Action:** Modal survey before cancellation page

```
Question 1: What's the main reason you're considering canceling?
• Too expensive
• Not using it enough
• Not seeing results
• Found alternative
• Other

Question 2: What would make you stay?
• Lower price
• Different features
• Better results
• More time to evaluate

Question 3: How can we improve?
[Open text box]
```

**Automated Analysis:** Immediate response based on answers

### Step 2: Targeted Retention Offer (Based on Response)

#### Scenario A: "Too expensive"
**Offer:** 1 month free + annual plan pitch
**Message:** "We value you! Enjoy a free month on us, then consider our annual plan at 35% off."

#### Scenario B: "Not using it enough"
**Offer:** Personalized onboarding restart
**Message:** "Let's get you started right! Here's a personalized 7-day jumpstart plan."

#### Scenario C: "Not seeing results"
**Offer:** Results accelerator package
**Message:** "Try our Sleep Breakthrough Protocol for 30 days, guaranteed improvement or cancel."

#### Scenario D: "Found alternative"
**Offer:** Feature comparison + trial extension
**Message:** "Compare features side-by-side. Take 14 more days to evaluate our exclusive tools."

#### Scenario E: "Other"
**Offer:** Flexible solution based on text analysis
**Message:** Automated response addressing specific concern

### Step 3: Downgrade & Legacy Options (If Retention Offer Declined)

#### Option A: Tools-Only Tier
**Price:** $9/month
**Features:** Basic tools only, no content/community
**Upsell Path:** Can upgrade back anytime

#### Option B: Legacy Access Tier (For 6+ Month Members)
**Eligibility:** Members with 6+ consecutive months of membership
**Price:** Free (read-only access)
**Features:**
- Read-only access to basic tools (Sleep Score Tracker, Environment Scanner)
- View historical data and progress
- Access to foundation content library
- Community read-only access
- No new tracking or personalized insights
- No tool updates or new features
**Benefits:**
- Preserves member's investment in data collection
- Maintains connection to sleep progress
- Creates potential re-activation path
- Builds goodwill and reduces cancellation friction
**Upsell Path:** One-click reactivation to full membership at any time

### Step 4: Cancellation Confirmation (If Proceed)
**Process:** Simple 1-click cancel (no tricks)
**Confirmation:** "We're sorry to see you go"
**Data Export:** 7-day window to download all data
**Goodbye Gift:** Free ebook "Maintaining Your Sleep Progress"

### Step 5: Win-back Sequence (Post-Cancellation)

#### Win-back Timeline
**Day 1:** "We miss you" + last chance to restore
**Day 7:** "Your data export expires soon"
**Day 30:** "What you've been missing" (progress others made)
**Day 60:** "Limited time reactivation offer"
**Day 90:** "New features you'd love" + special offer

#### Win-back Offers
**30-Day:** 50% off for 3 months
**60-Day:** 2 months free
**90-Day:** Annual plan at $99 (33% off normal annual)

## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
**Risk:** Automation fails to trigger  
**Mitigation:** Daily health checks + manual override

**Risk:** AI generates inappropriate responses  
**Mitigation:** Response templates + human review sampling

**Risk:** System overload at scale  
**Mitigation:** Cloud-native auto-scaling architecture

### Business Risks
**Risk:** Retention offers reduce revenue  
**Mitigation:** Value-based offers (not just discounts)

**Risk:** Cancellation flow causes frustration  
**Mitigation:** Simple 1-click final cancel + clear communication

**Risk:** Win-back offers train bad behavior  
**Mitigation:** Limited offers + increasing time between

### Compliance Risks
**Risk:** Subscription law violations  
**Mitigation:** Clear terms + easy cancel + no dark patterns

**Risk:** Data export failures  
**Mitigation:** Automated testing + backup manual process

**Risk:** Communication spam complaints  
**Mitigation:** Preference center + frequency caps
