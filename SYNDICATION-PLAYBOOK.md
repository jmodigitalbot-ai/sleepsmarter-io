## 1. Medium Syndication Strategy

### Best Practices for Medium Syndication

#### SEO & Canonical URLs
- **ALWAYS use canonical URLs**: When publishing on Medium, you MUST include a canonical link pointing back to the original article on SleepSmarter.io
- **Why this matters**: 
  - Prevents duplicate content penalties from Google
  - Tells search engines which version is the "original" source
  - Preserves SEO value for your main website
  - Medium supports `rel="canonical"` tags, unlike many other platforms

#### Formatting Tips for Medium
1. **Optimize headlines**: Make them catchy and engaging for Medium's audience
2. **Use Medium's formatting tools**: 
   - Subheadings (H2, H3) for scannability
   - Pull quotes for emphasis
   - Medium's native image formatting
3. **Add a featured image**: Medium articles with images get 94% more views
4. **Keep paragraphs short**: 2-3 sentences max for better readability
5. **Use bullet points and numbered lists**: Medium readers love scannable content

#### Best Tags for Sleep Content on Medium

## 4. Syndication Process for Every New Article

### Step-by-Step Workflow

**Week 1: Preparation**
- [ ] Write and publish article on SleepSmarter.io
- [ ] Wait 1-2 weeks for Google to index original content
- [ ] Identify 3-5 target Quora questions for this topic
- [ ] Prepare Medium-adapted version

**Week 2: Medium Syndication**
- [ ] Adapt article for Medium (use template)
- [ ] Set canonical URL to original article
- [ ] Add Medium-specific formatting and images
- [ ] Publish with 5 relevant tags
- [ ] Submit to relevant Medium publications
- [ ] Share from Medium account to social media

**Week 3: Quora Syndication**
- [ ] Answer 1-2 Quora questions per day (spread out)
- [ ] Use answer template with natural link to article
- [ ] Focus on high-traffic questions identified earlier
- [ ] Engage with comments on your answers
- [ ] Upvote helpful related answers

**Ongoing:**
- [ ] Monitor traffic from Medium and Quora (Google Analytics)
- [ ] Track which questions/answers perform best
- [ ] Update answer strategy based on performance
- [ ] Repeat for next article

### Tracking & Measurement

1. **Traffic**: Sessions from Medium and Quora (GA4)

3. **Engagement**: Medium claps, Quora upvotes
4. **SEO Impact**: Monitor original article rankings
