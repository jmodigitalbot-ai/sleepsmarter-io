## Image Style Guide

**Brand colors:**
- Primary: #a8dadc (soft teal)
- Background: #1a1a2e (dark navy)
- Accent: #f1faee (off-white)
- Text: #f1faee

**Style:**
- Clean, modern, minimal
- Soft gradients
- Simple illustrations over photos
- Dark mode friendly

**Typography in graphics:**
- Font: Inter or similar sans-serif
- Headers: Bold, larger
- Body: Regular weight
