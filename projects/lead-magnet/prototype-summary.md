# Personalized Sleep Blueprint - Interactive Prototype Summary

## Quick Overview
Transform the existing sleep calculator into an interactive assessment tool that delivers personalized sleep recommendations via ScoreApp or Involve.me.

## Core Components

### 1. Dynamic Assessment (12 Questions)
- **Sleep Patterns:** Wake time, bedtime, sleep latency, nighttime awakenings
- **Sleep Quality:** Morning refreshment, daytime sleepiness, primary challenges
- **Lifestyle:** Screen usage, caffeine, bedroom environment
- **Goals:** Primary objectives, time commitment

### 2. Personalized Recommendations Engine
- **Sleep Health Score (0-100):** Critical, Needs Work, Good, Excellent
- **Immediate Quick Wins:** Tonight's actionable steps
- **7-Day Sleep Reset Protocol:** Personalized day-by-day plan
- **Long-Term Optimization:** Circadian alignment, supplements, professional guidance

### 3. Value Proposition
"Most sleep advice is generic. Your sleep isn't. Get a blueprint designed for YOUR biology, YOUR schedule, and YOUR sleep challenges."

### 4. Design Mockup
- Progress-based assessment interface
- Visual score dashboard
- Personalized blueprint delivery
- Clean, sleep-focused design (blues, teals, whites)

### 5. Integration Options
**A. ScoreApp:** Built-in assessments, lead capture, PDF delivery
**B. Involve.me:** Interactive content, conditional logic, payments
**C. Custom Development:** Full control, seamless brand integration

## Implementation Phases

### Phase 1 (MVP - 2 weeks)
