## Gamification & Retention System

### Core Gamification Elements
1. **Streak Tracking**
   - Daily check-in streak
   - Weekly tool usage streak
   - Monthly achievement streak
   - Visual progress bars
   - Streak freeze (3/month for annual members)

2. **Achievement Badges**
   - **Foundation:** 7-day check-in streak
   - **Explorer:** Used all Level 1 tools
   - **Analyst:** 30 days of sleep tracking
   - **Optimizer:** Improved sleep score by 15%
   - **Scientist:** Completed 3 sleep experiments
   - **Sage:** Helped 5 community members

3. **Level Progression**
   - XP earned through:
     - Daily check-ins (10 XP)
     - Tool usage (25 XP/tool)
     - Content consumption (15 XP/article)
     - Community participation (20 XP/contribution)
   - Visual level-up animations
   - Unlock celebrations

4. **Community Leaderboards**
   - Weekly top performers
   - Monthly level leaders
   - All-time achievement holders
   - Anonymous display (opt-in)

### Retention Mechanics
1. **Sunk Cost Progression**
   - Visible progress bars
   - "Don't lose your streak" reminders
   - Achievement walls showing what's next

2. **Exclusive Tool Access**
   - Tools stop working upon cancellation
   - Data export offered (but tools disabled)
   - "We miss you" reactivation offers

3. **Annual Commitment Benefits**
   - Lower effective monthly rate
   - Streak freezes (3/month)
   - Early access to new tools
   - Annual achievement badge

4. **Cancellation Flow**
   - **Step 1:** "What's wrong?" survey
   - **Step 2:** Retention offer (1 month free)
   - **Step 3:** Pause membership option (see below)
   - **Step 4:** Downgrade option (tools-only tier)
   - **Step 5:** Data export + goodbye
   - **Step 6:** Win-back sequence (30/60/90 days)

5. **Pause Membership Feature**
   - **Availability:** All active members
   - **Duration Options:** 1, 2, or 3 months
   - **Frequency Limit:** Maximum 2 pauses per calendar year
   - **Data Preservation:** All progress, streaks, and data preserved during pause
   - **Billing:** Automatically resumes after pause period
   - **Access:** Tools temporarily disabled, content library remains accessible
   - **Notifications:** Email reminder 7 days before pause ends
   - **Benefits:** Prevents full cancellation during temporary life disruptions (travel, illness, busy periods)
