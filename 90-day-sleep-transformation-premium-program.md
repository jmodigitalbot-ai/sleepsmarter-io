### Step 1: Morning Light & Evening Dark (Daily)
**Morning (Within 30 minutes of waking):**

- Avoid screens for first 30 minutes if possible

**Evening (2 hours before bed):**
- Dim lights throughout your home
- Install blue light filters on all screens
- Use warm, low-wattage bulbs in bedroom

### Step 2: Consistent Sleep-Wake Times (Daily)
- Choose a consistent bedtime (within 30 minutes)
- Choose a consistent wake time (within 30 minutes)
- Set phone reminders for both times
- Stick to schedule 7 days/week (weekends included)

### Step 3: 10-Minute Wind-Down Routine (Nightly)
1. **Minute 1-3:** Turn off overhead lights, use lamp only
2. **Minute 4-6:** Write down tomorrow's 3 most important tasks
3. **Minute 7-9:** Gentle stretching or deep breathing
4. **Minute 10:** Get into bed, lights out

- **Week 1:** Implement all 3 steps 5+ days

- **Week 3:** Experience more consistent morning energy
