## Introduction: The Science of White Noise
White noise isn't just background sound—it's a scientifically proven sleep aid that masks disruptive environmental noises, helping you fall asleep faster and stay asleep longer. In our increasingly noisy world, white noise machines have become essential sleep technology.

## How White Noise Works
White noise creates a consistent sound that:
- Blocks sudden, jarring noises
- Reduces brain's alertness to environmental sounds
- Helps regulate sleep cycles
- Reduces stress and anxiety
- Beneficial for:
  - Light sleepers
  - Urban dwellers
  - People with tinnitus
  - Shift workers
  - Parents with young children

## Buying Guide: What to Consider

### Sound Quality
- Consistency of noise
- Volume range
- Natural vs. digital generation
- Variety of sound options

### Features to Evaluate
- Power source (battery/electric/USB)
- Size and portability
- Timer functions
- Additional features (light, alarm, etc.)

### Budget Considerations
- **Budget ($20-50):** Basic functionality
- **Mid-range ($50-100):** Enhanced features
- **Premium ($100+):** Smart technology integration

## Care and Maintenance
- Clean regularly with soft, dry cloth
- Avoid liquid exposure
- Store in cool, dry place
- Replace batteries promptly
- Update firmware for smart devices

## Health and Safety
- Use at moderate volume
- Position away from direct ear contact

- Not a substitute for medical advice
