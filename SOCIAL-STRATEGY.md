# SleepSmarter.io Social Media Strategy for Buffer

## 1. Buffer Free Plan Capabilities

Based on Buffer's current pricing (as of February 2026), the Free plan includes:

### Key Features:
- **3 social channels** (perfect for our priority platforms)
- **10 scheduled posts per channel** (refills when posts publish)
- **100 content ideas** storage
- **AI Assistant** for content refinement
- **Basic analytics** (30-day history)
- **Community inbox** for engagement
- **Visual content calendar**
- **Queue management**
- **Link shortening**
- **Browser extension**

### Limitations to Note:
- No hashtag manager (only 3 tags available)
- No first comment scheduling for Instagram
- No channel groups for bulk scheduling
- 30-day analytics history only
- 1 saved reply for community management

## 3. Content Strategy by Platform

### Pinterest Strategy:
**Frequency:** 3-5 pins per day
**Best Times:** 8-11 PM, 2-4 PM (when people plan/think about sleep)
**Content Types:**
- Infographics summarizing sleep tips
- Article graphics with key takeaways
- "Sleep hack" carousels
- Before/after sleep improvement visuals
- Product recommendation pins (affiliate focus)

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- Primary: #SleepTips, #BetterSleep, #SleepHacks
- Secondary: #SleepScience, #SleepWell, #HealthySleep
- Niche: #PinterestSleep, #SleepInfographic

**Image Recommendations:**
- Vertical orientation (2:3 or 4:5 ratio)
- Clear, readable text overlay
- Calming color palette (blues, purples, neutrals)
- Include website URL/logo subtly
- Alt text with keywords

### Twitter/X Strategy:
**Frequency:** 3-4 tweets per day (as per existing plan)
**Best Times:** 9 AM, 12 PM, 3 PM, 7 PM EST
**Content Types:**
- Quick sleep tips (280 characters max)
- Threads for deeper topics (Wednesday)
- Article shares with hooks
- Engagement questions
- Myth debunking
- Calculator promotions

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- 1-2 hashtags per tweet max
- Primary: #SleepTips, #SleepScience
- Rotating: #BetterSleep, #SleepHacks, #NewParent, #ShiftWork

**Character Limits:**
- Main tweets: 280 characters
- Threads: 5-8 tweets of 280 characters each
- Include link space (23 characters for shortened URL)

### Facebook Strategy:
**Frequency:** 1-2 posts per day
**Best Times:** 7-9 PM, 1-3 PM
**Content Types:**
- Longer-form sleep advice
- Personal stories/anecdotes
- Polls and questions
- Article shares with personal commentary
- Video content (short tips)
- Community-building posts

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- 3-5 hashtags max
- Broad: #Sleep, #Health, #Wellness
- Specific: #SleepTips, #InsomniaHelp, #ParentingSleep

**Post Length:**
- Optimal: 40-80 characters for highest engagement
- Can go longer for story-style posts
- Include clear call-to-action

## 5. Article-Based Post Strategy

Based on 10 published articles (plus upcoming content):

### Article Promotion Approach:
1. **Launch Post:** When article publishes
2. **Tip Extraction:** 2-3 posts pulling key tips from article
3. **Visual Summary:** Infographic/Pinterest version
4. **Engagement Hook:** Question based on article topic
5. **Evergreen Repromotion:** Every 4-6 weeks

### Post Types per Article:
1. **Hook Post** - Intriguing question/statistic
2. **Tip Post** - Actionable takeaway
3. **Visual Post** - Infographic/key points
4. **Calculator Post** - If applicable
5. **Question Post** - Engagement driver

## 6. Buffer Scheduling Workflow

### Weekly Workflow:
1. **Monday Morning:** Schedule week's content in Buffer
2. **Daily:** 10-minute engagement check/response
3. **Friday:** Review analytics, plan next week
4. **Sunday:** Create content for following week

### Buffer Queue Management:
- Maintain 10-post queue for each channel
- Schedule 7 days in advance
- Use "Best Time to Post" suggestions
- Mix content types throughout day
- Include weekend content (Pinterest especially)

### Content Creation Batch Process:
1. Create 20 posts bi-weekly
2. Design Pinterest images in batches
3. Write Twitter threads weekly
4. Schedule all at once in Buffer
