Troubleshooting Guide

If you miss your bedtime:

Use the next complete cycle option. For example, if you miss your 10:00 PM bedtime, aim for 11:30 PM (one full cycle later) rather than 10:30 PM.

Recovering from poor sleep:

Stick to your wake time the next morning, even if you slept poorly. This helps reset your circadian rhythm.
