### The 90-Minute Secret (Why This Works)
Your brain doesn't sleep in hours — it sleeps in 90-minute cycles. Each cycle includes:
- **Stage 1:** Light sleep (5-10 minutes)
- **Stage 2:** Deeper sleep (20 minutes)
- **Stage 3:** Deep sleep (30 minutes) — Physical restoration
- **Stage 4:** REM sleep (30 minutes) — Mental restoration

**The Critical Insight:** Waking up BETWEEN cycles = refreshed. Waking up DURING a cycle = groggy (sleep inertia).

## Part 4: Tonight's Quick Wins (Do These First)

### 1. The 4-7-8 Breathing Exercise
**How:** Inhale 4 seconds → Hold 7 seconds → Exhale 8 seconds → Repeat 4 times
**Why:** Activates parasympathetic nervous system, reduces heart rate by 15-20%
**Result:** Fall asleep 9-12 minutes faster

### 2. Strategic Temperature Drop
**How:** Take warm shower 90 minutes before bed, then let body cool
**Why:** Mimics natural temperature drop that triggers sleep
**Result:** 30% increase in deep sleep

### 3. "Brain Dump" Journaling
**How:** Write tomorrow's to-do list 60 minutes before bed
**Why:** Reduces cognitive arousal, stops mental rehearsal
**Result:** 42% reduction in nighttime awakenings

### 4. Progressive Muscle Relaxation
**How:** Tense and release each muscle group from toes to head
**Why:** Reduces physical tension, signals safety to nervous system
**Result:** Wake up with less muscle stiffness

### 5. Sleep-Position Optimization
